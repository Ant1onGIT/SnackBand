Learn More About Us‼

SnackBand was created by us to appease parents who were tired of messy snack bags and spills aplenty during car travel, playtime, and school recess. They wanted a fun, secure, and easy way for kids to carry small snacks — without needing extra containers or cleanup.

By combining the convenience of wearable gear with the excitement of snacking, SnackBand was born: a playful, child-sized wristband that holds just the right amount of snack — wherever your child may go.

Now, SnackBand is helping families snack smarter, cleaner, and more excitingly day after day.
